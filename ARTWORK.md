# Artwork direction

The hero environment was generated with the built-in OpenAI image-generation tool. The family-provided celebration-of-life references were used to establish the mother-and-son photo-booth memory. The surrounding arcade, tokens, lighting, cabinet silhouettes, and future-grid environment are generated artwork.

The production prompt was:

> Use both attached celebration-of-life handout images as source material. Locate the four-frame photo-booth sequence of the mother and her young son. Recompose that real photo-booth strip as the emotional centerpiece inside an elegant glowing arcade ticket-window frame, surrounded by an original 1986 arcade atmosphere transitioning subtly into a hopeful AI-era future. Preserve their identity, faces, expressions, age, hair, clothing, poses, and analog imperfections with high fidelity. Do not beautify, age, de-age, redraw, caricature, or invent either face. Use a wide 16:9 composition with dark negative space on the right for interface text. The mood is exuberant, rebellious, loving, electric, and warm rather than somber. Use midnight indigo, electric cyan, warm arcade amber, faded coral red, nickel silver, and restrained phosphor green. Include no text, logos, commercial characters, religious imagery, funeral imagery, or watermarks.

The generated source is preserved by Codex; the optimized website asset is `public/art/cathy-arcade-hero.jpg`. Family references and derived memorial art remain all rights reserved by the Thompson-Smith family and are excluded from the MIT source-code license.
